# MyFluidPal 💧

Welcome to **MyFluidPal**, your personal hydration companion.

MyFluidPal helps you build healthier hydration habits by tracking your daily water, coffee, and alcohol intake with an intuitive and easy-to-use interface.

## Features

- 💧 Water intake tracking
- ☕ Coffee tracking
- 🍺 Alcohol tracking
- 📊 Daily and weekly statistics
- 🔔 Smart hydration reminders
- 📈 Progress tracking
